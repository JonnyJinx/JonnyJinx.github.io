Features

Exploration

Traverse across open fields, mountains and deserts in a Hyrule of unmatched size. Ride horses, surf on your shield or glide through the air to new horizons. You may just find some treasure chests...

Ancient Shrines

Find and enter the mysterious shrines of Hyrule. These ancient structures are home to tricky traps, puzzles and ancient guardians. But if you can overcome these trials, rewards surely await.

Divine Beasts

The four Divine Beasts are ancient automatons that are home to the most difficult puzzles the world of Hyrule has to offer, along with some of Ganon's deadliest minions. You will need all your courage, and wisdom, to conquer these treacherous places.

Sheikah Slate

The Sheikah Slate will be your greatest guide in your journey. From providing a map for Link's journey to allowing him to use powerful Runes, the Sheikah Slate's usefulness is unrivaled.

Amiibos

Scan Amiibos to unlock bonus items to use on your quest. Use special Breath of the Wild amiibos to unlock special armour, weapons and other extras!
