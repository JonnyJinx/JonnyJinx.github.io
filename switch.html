Nintendo Switch

TV Mode

Use your Nintendo Switch Dock to connect your console to your TV to play in TV Mode! Grab your Joy-Con controllers and enjoy seeing beautiful vistas with a larger picture and more detail.

Handheld Mode

Take your adventure with you no matter where you go! Whether you're on a work break or at the park, the Nintendo Switch will always deliver high quality experiences. Simply remove your Nintendo Switch from the Nintendo Switch Dock and use the attached Joy-Con controllers to play!

Tabletop Mode

No TV nearby? No problem! Play your game directly from the Switch's screen by removing your Nintendo Switch from the Switch Dock and place it on a flat surface. Remove the Joy-Cons from the Dock and get playing!
