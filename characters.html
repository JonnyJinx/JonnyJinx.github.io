Characters

Link

The Hero of Hyrule. Link is Hyrule's strongest knight, who was taken to the Shrine of Resurrection to heal after being defeated by Calamity Ganon's forces. After sleeping for 100 years, Link finally awakens to challenge Ganon and save Princess Zelda, as well as the Kingdom of Hyrule.

Princess Zelda

The Princess of Hyrule, who has been locked in a hundred-year conflict with Calamity Ganon, and carries the blood of the ancient goddess, Hylia. While Zelda would rather read books and do research than fulfill an ancient prophecy, she dillegently plays her role nonetheless.

Mipha

Princess of the Zora people, Mipha is one of the four champions of Hyrule. She has a kind heart and is an adept healer, while also having great skill with the spear. Struck down by Calamity Ganon's forces 100 years ago, it now falls to Link to soothe her troubled spirit, along with those of the other champions.

Daruk

Another of the four champions, Daruk is a legendary Goron warrior. Despite his loud and brash nature, Daruk is a loyal friend of Link and an exceptionally powerful and brave warrior, who would gladly charge into battle against Ganon himself. Despite this, he has an unusual fear of dogs.

Revali

Revali is the champion from the Rito Tribe, a race of bird-like people who live in mountainous regions. Revali is the most skilled archer of his tribe, and makes sure to remind everyone of this fact. He has a rivalry with Link, and makes his skepticism of Link's worthiness as a hero known. Despite his attitude, when the time comes, Revali will play this part.

Urbosa

The former Gerudo Chief, and another of the four champions. Urbosa is a powerful and wise warrior, who shared a strong sisterly bond with Princess Zelda. Despite her good nature and kindness, Urbosa is not someone you would wish to disappoint, or make an enemy of.

Calamity Ganon

Calamity Ganon is the ancient incarnation of evil, and the being behind all of Hyrule's woes. Having all but completely conquered Hyrule 100 years in the past, Ganon's only desire is to defeat Princess Zelda and destroy the kingdom once and for all. Link alone can defeat Calamity Ganon and save Hyrule.
