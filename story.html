Story

The Hero Awakens

Our story begins in the Shrine of Resurrection. As Link, you awaken in an untamed world, ravaged by battle. Hearing the voice of Princess Zelda, Link is told that he has been asleep for one hundred years.

A Mysterious Old Man

In the Great Plateau, Link will encounter an old man living there. Was it by chance, or fate that this old man happened to be waiting right next to where Link awakens? And does the old man know more than he is letting on?

Memories of the Past

After one hundred years of slumber, Link has lost his memories. This does not mean that they cannot return, however. By visiting certain locations, you may be able to give Link back the memories he once had, of old friends and old places.

The Divine Beasts

On your journey, you will soon learn of the Divine Beasts, gigantic ancient machines that will prove pivotal to Link's quest. Can you locate and make your way into these great devices?

The Princess and the Castle

Princess Zelda faces the great evil, Calamity Ganon, alone in Hyrule Castle. The Princess may contain its power, but only with the help of Link can Ganon be vanquished once and for all. Can Link become the hero Hyrule needs once again, save Zelda and drive the evil from Hyrule?
